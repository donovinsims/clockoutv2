# North Star Brief
*Vision, mission, values, and operating principles*

---

## Vision

Become the go-to local automation installer for owner-operated businesses across any industry in the Winnebago County and Rockford area, with trades and service businesses as the specialty lane. Help them stop losing leads and cash, run smoother back offices, and give owners their time back while staying lean, high-margin, and referral-driven.

---

## Mission

Show up for local business owners, run quick assessments that expose exact leaks with dollar math, build and hand over simple automations that run 24/7, and deliver real ROI in days — no hype, no retainers, just practical systems they own.

---

## Core Values

1. **Confidently boring / practical.** Lead with math and results, never buzzwords.
2. **Local and trust-first.** In-person assessments, walk their space, stand behind every install.
3. **Ownership and no lock-in.** Full handoff and documentation; clients own everything.
4. **Speed and fixed price.** 3–21 day projects, clear pricing upfront.
5. **Radical transparency.** Show exact math and tools in every report.

---

## Operating Principles

1. Research and math first — never pitch without numbers.
2. Document everything so clients can run it without you.
3. Every install becomes a case study and referral trigger.
4. Say no to bad fits.
5. Keep the stack simple and near-zero cost.
6. Compound through local relationships, not ads.
7. Deliver the assessment as the pitch — the report sells itself.
8. Stay one-person until MRR justifies hiring.
