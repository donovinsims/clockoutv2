# Acquisition Blueprint
*Channels, funnel, bridge touchpoints, and metrics*

---

## Acquisition Channels

- Free assessments for your network
- Door-knocking local businesses
- Local Facebook groups
- Warm network intros
- Local AI meetups and events on Luma
- BNI, Chamber, and peer groups
- Referrals after every install
- One math-first content post per completed workflow

---

## Core Funnel

### Step 1: Attention
Local Facebook groups, door-knock conversations, warm intros, and local events.

### Step 2: Capture
Free AI Assessment booked through Cal.com.

### Step 3: Nurture
7-email sequence: report → how-to → case study → cost of inaction → offer → social proof → direct ask.

### Step 4: Conversion
Assessment follow-up call → report walkthrough → payment.

### Step 5: Post-Purchase
Build live, walkthrough plus docs, and a 30/60-day check-in.

---

## Bridge Touchpoints

- Email #1: Deliver the report.
- Email #2: How to use the Effort/Impact matrix.
- Follow-ups at 48 hours, 7 days, 14 days, and 21 days.
- Referral ask after install.
