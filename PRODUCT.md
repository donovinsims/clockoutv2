## Product
Clockout — local automation consultancy for owner-operated businesses across any industry. Trades and service businesses (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, contracting) are the specialty lane. Marketing website only. Monochrome, conversion-optimized. Fixed-price automation installation (7–21 days). Local-first: Winnebago County, IL area; remote for strong fits. Audience: Age 35–55. $500K–$3M revenue.

## Positioning
Not a SaaS. Not a remote agency. A single consultant who shows up in person, installs automation systems clients own outright, and leaves. Local trust is the differentiator.
