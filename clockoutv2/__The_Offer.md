## Entry: Free AI Assessment

**Price:** Free (→ $150–$250 once you have testimonials)
**Time:** 20–30 minute conversation (phone or in-person)
**Deliverable:** Written report with 3–5 specific automations and estimated monthly ROI for each

This is the close, not the opener. After 20 minutes of real conversation, you understand their business better than any agency that's pitched them. The assessment *is* the pitch.

**What the report includes:**

- **Executive summary** — their pain points in their own words
- **Effort vs. Impact matrix** — maps each pain point by how hard it is to fix vs. how much it's worth. Focus is on quick wins: low effort, high impact
- **Recommended solutions** — 3–5 specific tools or workflows, explained in plain English with cost and ROI
- **4-Day Quick Win Plan** — day-by-day steps so they can act immediately, even without you
- **What's next** — 1–2 bigger items worth solving together, planted as the natural upsell

**ROI framing template:**
> *"You're losing an estimated $[X]/month to [problem]. This workflow recovers [Y]% of that. Setup is $[Z]. It pays for itself in [weeks]."*

**Conversion rule:** Every assessment ends with one clear next step. Offer the Single Fix for the highest-pain item. If 3+ problems surface, offer the bundle.

## Offer 1: Single System Fix

**Best for:** One specific, high-pain problem
**Timeline:** 3–7 days
**Setup fee:** $300–$800
**Monthly monitoring:** $20–$40/mo (optional)

### What's Included
- Assessment call to confirm the specific problem
- Build and deployment of one targeted workflow
- Testing and QA
- Simple documentation
- 30-day check-in

### Flagship Workflows

**Missed Call Text-Back — $500 setup**
Call goes unanswered → SMS fires within 60 seconds → routes replies to owner → books appointments.
*Best for: HVAC, plumbers, contractors.*
→ *At $400/avg job, recovering 2 leads/week = $3,200/mo. Setup pays back in 5 days.*

**Review Request Automation — $400 setup**
24 hours after job completion → SMS with direct Google review link → one follow-up → tracks who reviewed.
→ *10x more happy customers than current reviews. Every 10 new reviews = 2–3 more leads/mo.*

**Estimate Follow-Up Sequence — $400 setup**
Estimate sent → auto follow-up at 48hrs → 5-day follow-up if no response → owner alert at day 7.
→ *30–40% of cold estimates can be recovered with one follow-up message.*

**Invoice Follow-Up — $500 setup**
At 3, 7, and 14 days overdue: automated reminders with payment link, increasing urgency. Owner alert at day 14.
→ *Reduces late payments by 60%. Faster cash flow, fewer awkward calls.*

**No-Show Recovery — $500 setup**
No-show detected → 10-min wait → SMS with 3 rebooking slots → one follow-up at 2 hours.
→ *Recovers 30–40% of lost appointments. At $150/avg service call, 2 recovered/week = $1,200/mo.*

## Pricing Matrix

| Tier | Included | Setup Fee | Monthly |
|---|---|---|---|
| Assessment | Written ROI report, effort/impact matrix | Free → $150–$250 | — |
| Single Fix | 1 workflow, 1 integration | $300–$800 | $20–$40 |
| Operations Stack | 3–5 workflows, 2–4 integrations | $1,200–$3,000 | $40–$75 |

**Pricing rules:**
1. **Price by value, not time.** You build in 5 minutes. Price by what problem it solves.
2. **Anchor to their problem cost first.** Ask what it's costing them. Then your price is the obvious math.
3. **Bundle aggressively.** Three workflows at $1,500 always converts better than one at $500.
4. **The monitoring fee is real margin.** $40/mo × 20 clients = $800/mo recurring.
5. **Ramp with proof.** Start free, raise the assessment fee as testimonials come in.

## The Tool Stack (Near-Zero Cost)

Your tools cost near nothing. Your value is knowing which one solves which problem and how to connect them.

**Free / open-source core:**
- **n8n** (self-hosted) — workflow engine, free to run on a $5/mo VPS
- **Cal.com** — appointment booking, open source
- **Google Sheets** — logging, dashboards, free
- **Fathom** — meeting notes and transcripts, free tier
- **Make.com** — simple automations, free tier

**Pay-per-use (very cheap):**
- **Twilio** — SMS, pennies per message
- **GoHighLevel** — CRM + phone (~$97/mo, spread across clients)

A 3-workflow contractor stack might cost you $5–$15/mo to run. Your $1,500 setup is nearly pure margin. Clients don't need to know the stack — they need to know the result.

## The Close (One Script)

Ask: *"What's the most annoying part of running the business day-to-day?"*

Shut up. Write down their exact words.

Repeat the problem back. Show them the math. Build it in front of them if you can.

> *"This runs 24/7. You never touch it. Setup is $[X]. If you want me to keep an eye on it, that's $[Y]/month. Want me to leave it running?"*

No proposals. No decks. They just watched it work.

## Objection Cheat Sheet

**"That seems expensive."**
"How much is [problem] costing you per month? [Wait.] So this pays for itself in [X weeks]."

**"I need to think about it."**
"Totally fair. Every day it's not running, you're losing roughly $[X]. Price is the same this week."

**"Can you show me how to do it myself?"**
"When it breaks at 11pm and a lead is waiting — who fixes it? You're paying for reliability, not the build."

**"I already have someone for this."**
"What are you paying them? How fast do they respond when something breaks? This runs 24/7 and never calls in sick."
