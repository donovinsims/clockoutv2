## Email Examples

- **Email #1:** "Here's your custom report — your missed call workflow alone is costing you about $2,400/mo. Here's the Effort/Impact matrix and 4-Day Quick Win Plan..."
- **Email sequence structure:** deliver report → how-to → case study → cost of inaction → offer → social proof → direct ask.

## Objection Handling

- **"Seems expensive"** → "Totally fair. At your average deal size, recovering just 2 extra calls/week pays for the setup in 5 days. Here's the exact math for your volume."
- **"I need to think about it"** → "Totally fair. Every day it's not running, you're losing roughly $[X]. Price is the same this week."
- **"I already have someone for this"** → "What are you paying them? How fast do they respond when something breaks? This runs 24/7 and never calls in sick."

## Voice Rules

- **Tone:** Direct, local, anti-hype, confidently boring, practitioner.
- **Sentence style:** Short. Simple. Math-heavy. One clear next step.
- **Phrasing patterns:** "Here's the math…" / "This pays for itself in…" / "I show up, build it, hand it over" / "You own everything" / "No retainer, no hype" / "The leaks are the same everywhere — the fix is simple."
- **Use:** "Boring but it works" / "runs while you sleep" / specific dollar amounts / "fixed, fast, handed over" / "I show up in person."
- **Avoid:** "Revolutionary" / "game-changing" / "synergy" / "AI-powered transformation" / "unlock your potential."
